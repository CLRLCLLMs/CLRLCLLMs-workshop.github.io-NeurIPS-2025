CLRLCLLMs Workshop @ NeurIPS 2025

Centering Low-Resource Languages and Cultures in the Age of Large Language Models

Welcome to the CLRLCLLMs Workshop! The workshop at NeurIPS 2025 focuses on advancing inclusive AI research for low-resource languages and cultural representation.

Date: December 1, 2025

Location: Mexico City, Mexico

Room: Don Alberto 1

Overview

The CLRLCLLMs Workshop aims to foster dialogue and collaboration among researchers, practitioners, and policymakers dedicated to improving the inclusion of low-resource languages and cultures in large language model development. As LLMs increasingly shape the way people interact with technology, this workshop provides a space to highlight challenges, share resources, and explore ethical, cultural, and technical pathways toward linguistic and cultural inclusivity.

Through invited talks, paper presentations, and panel discussions, participants will examine the intersections of AI, linguistics, and cultural diversity ‚Äî promoting innovations that empower underrepresented language communities globally.
