Overview

The CLRLCLLMs Workshop at NeurIPS 2025 explores how large language models can be adapted to support low-resource languages and cultural representation. Our mission is to advance inclusive AI research that recognizes the linguistic and cultural diversity of our world.

Call for Participation

We invite researchers, linguists, developers, and community organizers working on low-resource language technologies, cultural data, and multilingual AI to participate. Share your insights, tools, or datasets that promote language and cultural inclusion in the age of LLMs.
